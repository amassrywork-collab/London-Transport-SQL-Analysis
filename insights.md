# Insights & Findings

After analyzing Transport for London (TfL) data (2010–2022):

1️⃣ **Most Popular Transport Modes**  
Buses and Underground & DLR dominate public mobility in London.

2️⃣ **Emirates Air Line Cable Car Insights**  
Usage peaks mainly during tourism seasons.  
It is not a primary commuting option.

3️⃣ **Tube Ridership Declines**  
Worst years: 2020–2022 due to pandemic restrictions.  
This highlights the sensitivity of mobility to external factors.
